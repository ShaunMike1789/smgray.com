Myo-Reps Trainer

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

Back to SMGray.com

Myo-Reps Trainer is a local-first workout logging app for planning and tracking Myo-Reps style training. The app is designed to work on your Android device and does not require you to create an account.

Information We Collect

Myo-Reps Trainer does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal information to the developer or to a remote server.

The app does not provide:

  • user accounts
  • advertising
  • analytics
  • in-app purchases
  • cloud sync
  • social features

Information Stored On Your Device

Myo-Reps Trainer stores workout information locally on your device using SQLite so the app can remember:

  • workout templates
  • built-in and custom exercise entries
  • active workout progress
  • completed workout history
  • progression and appearance settings

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Permissions

The Android app bundle currently includes Internet access through the Expo and React Native runtime. The app itself does not use a remote backend, account service, analytics service, ads service, or cloud sync.

Data Sharing

Myo-Reps Trainer does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Children

Myo-Reps Trainer is a general fitness logging app and is not directed specifically to children.

Data Deletion

You can remove locally stored app data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage from Android system settings.

Changes To This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app's features change. If the app later adds analytics, accounts, cloud sync, or any remote data collection, this policy must be updated before release.
