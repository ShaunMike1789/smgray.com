Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 10, 2026

Cat Litter Nag is a simple reminder app. This app is designed to work locally on your Android device and does not require you to create an account.

Information We Collect

Cat Litter Nag does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to a remote server.

The app does not provide:

  • user accounts
  • advertising
  • analytics
  • in-app purchases
  • cloud sync
  • social features

Information Stored On Your Device

Cat Litter Nag stores reminder settings locally on your device so the app can remember:

  • your selected reminder cadence
  • the next scheduled reminder time
  • the current reminder or snooze state

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Permissions

Cat Litter Nag may request notification permission on supported Android versions. This permission is used only to show reminder notifications.

Data Sharing

Cat Litter Nag does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Children

Cat Litter Nag is a general-purpose household reminder app and is not directed specifically to children.

Changes To This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app's features change. If the app later adds analytics, accounts, cloud sync, or any remote data collection, this policy must be updated before release.
