The common thread

Narrow products with obvious value.

The domains do not need to match. The work does: ship practical software, make the first screen useful, and give each product its own voice.

1
Start with a real task

Each app is organized around what people are trying to do, not around a generic portfolio category.

2
Keep the interface direct

Retirement planning, dashboards, and reminder utilities all benefit from clear next actions.

3
Let the products stand apart

SMGray is the maker behind the work; each application is free to serve its own audience.
